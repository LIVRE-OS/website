Solivre — Identity Kernel

Solivre is the identity-native kernel at the heart of LIVRE OS. It manages your claims, keys, permissions, and proof descriptors without ever putting raw identity data on-chain.

What Solivre manages

  • Identity root commitments
  • Claims (age, residency, qualifications, membership)
  • Permission policies & consent
  • Proof descriptors for GC/MPC
  • Bindings between identity and LivreID

Why it matters

Instead of scattering your data across institutions, Solivre gives you a single sovereign identity engine that can answer questions about you — cryptographically — without ever leaking your underlying information.
