LivreVault - Sovereign Storage

LivreVault is the encrypted storage layer for LIVRE OS. It lets you keep documents, certificates, metadata, and recovery materials under your control, encrypted locally, stored across nodes, and committed on-chain via hashes.

What you can store

  • Passports, IDs, residency proofs
  • Academic and professional certificates
  • KYB/KYC documents
  • Encrypted recovery shards
  • App-specific identity data

Security model

Everything is encrypted on your device before it moves anywhere. Nodes only see encrypted blobs and cannot decrypt them. On-chain, only hashed commitments are stored for integrity and optional proof references.
