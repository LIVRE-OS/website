LivreID

LivreID is the naming layer of LIVRE OS: human-readable identities like odin.livre that resolve to private identity anchors instead of public profiles.

Features

  • Simple, human-readable .livre names.
  • Names resolve to identity commitments, not raw data.
  • No global enumeration or listing of all names.
  • Optional aliases for different apps and contexts.

Privacy model

Resolving a LivreID never returns personal information. It only exposes the minimal cryptographic anchor needed for Solivre and the Proof Engine to operate.

No claims, no documents, and no app history are exposed by default — just enough structure for proofs to bind to a stable, user-controlled identity.
