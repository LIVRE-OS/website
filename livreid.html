LivreID

LivreID is the naming layer of LIVRE OS: human-readable identities like odin.livre that resolve to private identity anchors instead of public profiles.

Features

  • Simple, human-readable `.livre` names
  • Names resolve to identity commitments, not data
  • No enumeration or listing of all names
  • Optional aliases for different contexts

Privacy model

Resolving a LivreID never returns personal information. It only returns the minimal cryptographic anchor needed for Solivre and the Proof Engine to operate. No claims, no documents, no app history are exposed.
